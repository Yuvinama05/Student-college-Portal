Welcome To Student-College Portal

A Student-College Portal is a web-based platform that helps colleges take student data online for easier management and better clarity. That's at its most basic.

This system is able to collect college-wide data online so that it can be easily accessed by teachers, parents, students, and administrators. That includes personal student information, grades, records of tests, attendance, and plenty more.

Essentially, this allows the college to make data points for lots of areas in one place so that it's easy to keep track of progress and performance.

To be clear, it's an SIS(Student Information System) we're talking about here, which can also break down into a Student Management System (SMS), Student Information Management System (SIMS), or Student Records System (SRS) - all created to help keep records digitally.
